About Me

A graduate from Kutztown University with a degree in Pure Mathematics, Jordan Smith is an academically trained problem solver and logical thinker. For the past 7 years post-graduation, Jordan has utilized this background in the field of Sales and Marketing.

Jordan has worked for Barnes and Noble (Nook Sales/Technician), Mondelez Inc. (Nabisco Sales Representative), and most recently, Market Resource Partners in Philadelphia, PA. During his time there, Jordan managed multitdue of campaigns for differenct clients including Google, Oracle, HPE, Splunk, and Veritas.

Outside of work, Jordan enjoys reading comics, working on his podcast, playing music with his best friends, and watching the Sixers continue their quest for an NBA Championship.
